About Me

Dorian Frank Porter was born and raised in Memphis, TN. After accepting a position as a baker at Vanderbilt University in 2014, He relocated to Nashville,TN where he has since remained.
In 2017,he sought further education in business, enrolling in American Intercontinental University majoring in Business Administration focused on Entrepreneurship. He is also an associate of Music City Taxes and is very involved on assisting the public with their tax filing needs on an annual basis.
